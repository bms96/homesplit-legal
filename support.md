## Reporting a bug

To help us fix it fast, please include:

- What you were doing, and what happened instead.
- Your device (e.g. iPhone 15) and your iOS or Android version.
- A screenshot, if you have one.

The easiest way is to report it straight from the app: **Household → Settings → Report a bug**. It pre-fills your app version and device details for you.

---

## Common questions

**How do I cancel HomeSplit Pro?**
Manage or cancel anytime in your device settings: **Apple ID → Subscriptions** (iOS) or **Google Play → Subscriptions** (Android). Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

**How do I delete my account?**
In the app: **Household → Settings → Delete Account**.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. See our [Privacy Policy](/homesplit-legal/) for exactly what we collect and why.
