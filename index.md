# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data HomeSplit collects, why we collect it, and how it is handled. It applies to the HomeSplit mobile application on iOS and Android, which is operated by **Bryan Santini, an individual sole proprietor doing business as HomeSplit** ("we," "us," "the app").

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at **bryansantini@yahoo.com**.

---

## What data we collect

We only collect what's needed to make HomeSplit work for you. We do not sell your data, we do not run ads, and we do not collect anything we don't actively use.

### Account information

When you sign in, we collect:
- **Email address** — used to authenticate you and to send magic-link sign-in messages.
- **Display name** — what other roommates in your household see.
- **Phone number** *(optional)* — only if you choose to add it. We do not send marketing texts.

### Household data

When you create or join a household, we store:
- **Household name** and **address** *(optional)*.
- **Membership** — which members belong to which household, when they joined, and (if applicable) when they moved out.
- **Member display info** — name, accent color, and admin/member role.

### Bills, expenses, and balances

To run the core feature, we store:
- **Recurring bills** you create (rent, utilities, etc.) — name, amount, frequency, due date, split rules.
- **One-time expenses** — amount, description, category, who paid, who owes what.
- **Settlement records** — when one roommate pays another back, we record the amount, method (Venmo, CashApp, cash, other), date, and any notes you add.
- **Move-out records** — when a roommate leaves, the prorated settlement and final balances.

We do **not** collect credit card numbers, bank account information, or transaction details from any third-party payment service. Settle-ups happen outside HomeSplit through links to Venmo or Cash App; we do not see, store, or process the actual payment.

### Subscription information

If you subscribe to HomeSplit Pro:
- Your purchase is processed by **Apple** (App Store) or **Google** (Play Store). We never see your payment details.
- We receive a subscription status (active, expired, etc.) from **RevenueCat**, our subscription management provider, so we know whether to unlock Pro features.

### Device and notification data

- **Push notification token** — generated by Expo's push service. We use it to send notifications about due bills, settlement reminders, and household activity. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.
- **Device platform** (iOS or Android) — to deliver platform-appropriate notifications.

### Usage analytics

We use **PostHog** to understand how the app is used so we can improve it. This includes:
- Anonymized event data (which screens you view, which actions you take).
- A device-level user identifier so PostHog can track sessions over time.
- App version, OS version, device model.

We do **not** send PostHog any of your bills, expenses, balances, or other personally sensitive content.

### Crash and error reports

We use **Sentry** to capture errors and crashes so we can fix bugs. Sentry collects:
- Stack traces and error messages from the app.
- Device platform, OS version, and app version.
- Anonymized identifiers to group similar crashes.

Sentry does not receive your bills, expenses, balances, or message content.

## How we use your data

We use your data only to:
1. Authenticate you and keep you signed in.
2. Run the core HomeSplit features — bills, expenses, balances, move-outs, settle-ups.
3. Send you push notifications about activity in your household (you can disable these).
4. Process and verify your Pro subscription.
5. Improve the app by understanding how it's used and fixing bugs.

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your data with anyone outside the third-party processors listed below.

---

## Who we share your data with

HomeSplit stores and processes data through these third-party services. We've selected each because they meet industry standards for data protection.

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** | Auth, database, file storage | Email, household and member data, bills, expenses, settlements |
| **RevenueCat** | Subscription state | Subscription identifier, subscription status |
| **Apple App Store / Google Play** | Subscription billing | Whatever Apple/Google needs to bill you (we don't see payment details) |
| **PostHog** | Product analytics | Anonymized usage events |
| **Sentry** | Error reporting | Crash reports, device/OS metadata |
| **Expo** | Push notifications | Device push tokens, notification payloads |

We do not share your data with any other third parties, including advertisers, data brokers, or marketing networks.

## Data retention

We keep your data as long as your account exists. If you delete your account or move out of all households:
- Your account record is deleted from our database.
- Bills, expenses, and settlements you participated in are retained as part of your former household's history (so other members can still see what happened), but your personal information is anonymized.
- Backups may retain data for up to 30 days before being permanently purged.

## Your rights

Regardless of where you live, you can:
- **Access your data** — contact us and we'll send you a copy.
- **Correct your data** — most fields are editable directly in the app.
- **Delete your account** — go to the Household tab → Settings → Delete Account, or email us.
- **Opt out of analytics** — contact us and we'll exclude your device.

If you live in the **European Union** (GDPR) or **California** (CCPA/CPRA), you have additional rights including the right to object to processing, the right to data portability, and the right to file a complaint with your local data protection authority. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

## Children's privacy

HomeSplit is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU) and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we'll delete it.

---

## Security

We use HTTPS for all network traffic. Authentication is handled by Supabase using industry-standard JWT tokens. Sensitive credentials (your auth session) are stored on your device using the operating system's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). We use row-level security in our database to ensure roommates can only see data for households they belong to.

No system is 100% secure. If you suspect your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

---

## International data transfers

HomeSplit's servers and third-party processors are located primarily in the United States. If you use HomeSplit from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and stored in the US. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.
